# Specialty Research Lenses

Use these lenses to search for role-aligned AI learning resources without asking for sensitive mission, unit, location, system, clearance, or operational information.

These are search and evaluation lenses, not required course titles. A good public course may use civilian, academic, commercial, or public-sector terminology while still filling one of these capability gaps.

## Intelligence, ISR, Research, And Analysis

Search for resources that support:

- Source verification
- Provenance
- Hallucination and misinformation detection
- Structured analytic techniques
- Geospatial and imagery workflows
- Data fusion
- Bias and uncertainty
- Human-machine analytic teaming
- Briefing and product development
- Appropriate human review

Potential mapping:

- Professional fields: `Intelligence, research, and analysis`; `Software development, data, and AI`
- Work patterns: `analyze-information`; `create-communicate`; `plan-decide`
- Learning goals: `research-analysis`; `evaluate-outputs`; `work-with-data`; `writing-communication`
- Tags: `verification`; `analysis`; `provenance`; `geospatial`; `bias`; `uncertainty`
